About Me

You can call me Didi, I'm a

I am the creative force behind D'seikou, a multimedia design brand committed to bringing your creative vision to life in a unique and compelling way. The name D'seikou is derived from my surname, Diseko, and the Japanese word for success, 'Seikou.' With a passion for visual communication, we create visually stunning designs that engage, inspire, and leave a lasting impression. Our subsidiary, Kustom Kreations, specializes in customizing sneakers and tufted rugs to create unique, one-of-a-kind pieces. We are dedicated to helping you achieve your creative goals and making your dreams a reality. With our vision to reality motto and outstanding customer service, let's work together to create something amazing.
